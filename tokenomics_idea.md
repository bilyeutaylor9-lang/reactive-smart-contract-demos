AI Sentinel Tokenomics

Overview

AI Sentinel is designed around a sustainable utility model where token demand is generated through automation, intelligence services, and protocol security rather than speculative emissions.

The AI Sentinel token (AIS) serves as the economic foundation of the network, powering autonomous execution, risk intelligence, validator incentives, governance participation, and future AI-driven automation marketplaces.

Utility

AIS powers every layer of the AI Sentinel ecosystem.

Automation Fees

Users pay small AIS fees when automated actions are executed.

Examples:

* Liquidation Protection
* Debt Repayment
* Collateral Management
* Portfolio Rebalancing
* Governance Automation
* Cross-Chain Execution
* Whale Intelligence Alerts

The more automation the network performs, the greater the utility demand for AIS.

⸻

Burn Mechanism

Every automation fee is distributed as follows:

50% Burn
30% Treasury
20% Staking Rewards

Example:

100 AIS Fee

* 50 AIS permanently burned
* 30 AIS sent to treasury
* 20 AIS distributed to stakers

This creates a deflationary model directly tied to network usage.

⸻

Staking

AIS holders may stake tokens to secure future intelligence and automation services.

Benefits include:

* Staking Rewards
* Governance Rights
* Reduced Automation Fees
* Early Feature Access
* Validator Participation

⸻

AI Risk Oracle Network

Future versions of AI Sentinel will introduce decentralized risk validators.

Validators:

* Analyze protocol risk
* Score market conditions
* Submit risk assessments
* Validate automation decisions

Validators earn AIS rewards for accurate participation.

Malicious or inaccurate submissions may result in slashing penalties.

⸻

Intelligence Marketplace

Developers will be able to publish AI modules and automation strategies.

Examples:

* Whale Monitoring Models
* Liquidation Prediction Models
* Governance Intelligence Modules
* Protocol Risk Models
* Portfolio Optimization Engines

Revenue generated through subscriptions will be distributed:

70% Module Creator
20% Treasury
10% Burn

Governance

AIS holders will govern protocol upgrades including:

* Risk Model Parameters
* Fee Structures
* Treasury Allocation

* Supported Chains
* New Automation Modules

⸻

Long-Term Vision

AI Sentinel aims to become the decentralized intelligence layer for Web3.

The long-term objective is to create a network where autonomous agents continuously monitor blockchain ecosystems, evaluate risk, predict threats, and execute intelligent actions across multiple protocols and chains.

AIS serves as the economic engine powering that ecosystem.

As adoption grows, automation usage increases, intelligence services expand, and token supply decreases through continuous protocol burns.

This creates a sustainable ecosystem where network growth directly benefits participants while strengthening the long-term value proposition of AIS.
